GELFANCO LTD (previously known as GLYKERIOS ESTATES LTD) was founded in 2001 by Glykerios Soteriou and constitutes a fast growing land development company, proud of the reputation it has gained over time for its quality and reliability in sales, delivery and title-registering of houses and apartments in the area of ​​Pervolia - Kiti Larnaca.

Our Company has worked hard to associate its name with excellence, quality, loyalty, and reliability. The company has so far completed 7 projects, and overall, 46 residences of utmost standards all of which have been granted titles.

In our page you can find out about the company’s history and our new projects.

CEO, Founder & Chairman of the Board

Glykerios founded the company in 2001 and managed to grow it to the company it is today. He has a proven 20-year track record of excellent customer satisfaction. He studied Business Administration.
