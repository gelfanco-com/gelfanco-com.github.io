Gelfanco 8 Court is a new residential project in Kiti by GELFANCO LTD Property Developers and it consists of 6 apartments.

Located at Samos street in a quiet area in Kiti, is characterized by its immediate access to the Softades beach, Careta beach, and the Faros beach at Pervolia, which is awarded with a blue flag by the EU for its clean waters. The project is shy of a kilometer away from the Kiti square and very close to the Kiti shopping streets, the supermarkets, and the schools. It is a 5-minute drive from Larnaca’s airport and has immediate access to the highway towards Nicosia and Limassol.

The project consists of 6 spacious apartments: 4 two-bedroom apartments and 2 three-bedroom ones. There will be 1 apartment on the ground floor, 3 on the first, and 2 apartments on the second floor. Each apartment will benefit from a dedicated parking bay as well as a storage room on the ground floor.

The project construction is starting soon and is expected to be completed before the end of 2022.

GELFANCO Off-Plan Investments

As a great means of investing, many people choose to buy off-plan properties or under construction ones. To buy such a property, a small amount of money is usually paid up-front, as a deposit, while the property is under construction and the rest of the payment is paid in stages (as agreed) until the completion of the construction. At the early stage of construction, the prices are greatly cheaper and a lot more competitive, so an investment like this is a great idea if you are looking to increase your capital in the short term, while simultaneously minimizing the initial price. The buyer gets the property at a discounted price, which can be later sold for a profit, either after of before its completion.

Another reason why investments in off-plan properties are successful is because only 30% is paid and the remaining 70% is given as a loan from our company-associated bank, with the property mortgaged. When you purchase an off-plan property you are not only getting your dream home, but we are also giving you the opportunity to style your property the way you had envisioned it.

Our team is always there, willing to discuss your investment needs, and help you make the right investment.

Financing under mortgage loans

Commercial banks in Cyprus are authorized to offer help in the purchase of an off-plan property with a mortgage loan to Cypriot or foreign applicants. The loan can be granted in euros or a foreign currency (e.g. USD, GBP, etc.). For a loan, with the property under mortgage, the maximum repayment period can be 25 years. The maximum loan amount is 70% of the value of the bought property. The loan is repaid with monthly installments.

The financial institutions may require the following information:

  • Property Sales Agreement
  • Customer’s profile
  • Passport/ID copies
  • Statement of existing debt (if any)
  • A reference Letter from the Banks you cooperate with
  • Completion of the attached Declaration of Personal Income
  • Evidence of income
  • Bank statements copies for the last three months
  • Any other statements the bank might require
Upon request, our company can advise customers who wish to buy an off-plan property under mortgage. For more information, do not hesitate to contact us.
